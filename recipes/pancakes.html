Pancakes

A deliscious breakfast

Try those fluffy and tender pancakes that will melt inside your mouth.

Easy to prepare and a great option to eat together with fruits or any syrup.

Ingredients

  • 150gr wheat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 25g white sugar
  • 50g warm unsalted butter
  • 200ml milk
  • 12g baking powder
  • A few drops of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Garnish: chocolate syrup, mapple syrp, honey, fruits, whipped cream...

Steps

  1. Beat milk, eggs, sugar and vanilla extract and warmed butter together in a bowl.
  2. Add flour, baking soda and a pinch of salt and mix well using a blender.
  3. Mid heat a buttered pan.
  4. Scoup the batter one by one and cook until bubbles begin to form on the top. Turn the pancake and repeat the same until all the pancakes are golden brown.
  5. Serve in a plate and add your favourite garnish.
